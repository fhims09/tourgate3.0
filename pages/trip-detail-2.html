🎖 Immersive 4 DAY · 3 NIGHT

4 Day Trip

Spend two full days with the Mentawai tribe, gaining deeper cultural insights and stronger connections with local families.

Photo Gallery

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Your 4-Day Journey

06:00

Pickup from your hotel in Padang.

07:00

Mentawai Fast speedboat departs for Sikabaluan, north of Siberut.

11:00

Arrive in Sikabaluan, North Siberut. Rest and have lunch here.

12:00

Continue from Sikabaluan to Siberut by Mentawai Fast transport.

13:00

Arrive in Siberut, our starting point for the trek. We continue by motorbike to Muntai.

14:00

Collect your Siberut National Park entry permit (included in the trip), then leave Muntai for Rorogot by motor canoe — about 1.5 hours.

16:30

Begin trekking from Rorogot to the Mentawai house — about 1.5 hours through the jungle.

18:30

Arrive at the Mentawai house and greet the tribe with "Anai lo ita." Enjoy tea or coffee and rest for the night.

At this point you will have 2 days to do all of these activities and will have more time to connect with the tribe

👣

We follow the Mentawai activity.

🌿

Going the jungle with shaman to explore jungle.

🥥

Watch the Mentawai woman make sago (food).

🎣

Go fishing in the traditional Mentawai way.

🏡

Your guide will explain all of the things inside the Uma (house).

🏹

Your guide will explain all about the semi-nomadic hunter-gatherer lifestyle of the tribe.

🔥

Every night we will be together with the tribe people and can talk with them about their way of life.

—

Time to say goodbye to your new friends — "Anan kai mei" (goodbye in Mentawai language).

08:30

After breakfast, trek back to the river where we arrived and take the boat back. Have lunch and wait for the boat to Padang.

14:30

Fast boat departs from Siberut to Padang.

18:30

Arrive in Padang. Transport back to your hotel.

Good to Know

What to Bring

CLOTHING & GEAR

  • 🎒Small backpack (you can leave extra luggage at your hotel/hostel in Padang)
  • 👖Long trousers or adventure pants, long sleeves, t-shirts, sun hat
  • 🧥Rain jacket or poncho
  • 🎒A dry bag to protect your camera, documents, and electronics
  • 👡Sandals for around the house
  • 👢Rubber boots (can also be bought in Siberut)

PERSONAL UTILITIES

  • 🛏️Sleeping bag
  • 🦟Mosquito repellent
  • ☀️High protection sunscreen
  • 🔦Headlamp
  • 🧻Toilet paper
  • 🧢Sun hat / cap & sunglasses
  • 💧Water bottle
  • 💊First aid: paracetamol, painkillers, anti-diarrhea, antiseptic cream, and any medication you usually take

Do not overpack — keep it simple. 🌿
