Day-by-Day Itinerary

Your 5-Day Journey

07:00

Meet your group and guide Aman at Padang's Minangkabau Airport. Brief orientation over coffee.

10:00

Ferry from Muara Padang to Muara Siberut — 3 hours across the Mentawai Strait. Dolphins common.

13:30

Arrive Siberut. Traditional welcome lunch at the village. Rest at the local guesthouse.

18:00

Evening gathering with the village headman. Learn the Sakkudei greeting customs. Dinner under the stars.

06:00

Early breakfast. Aman briefs the group on jungle etiquette and what to expect.

07:00

4-hour jungle trek to a remote interior uma (longhouse). Spot rare birds and medicinal plants along the way.

12:00

Arrive at uma. Lunch prepared by village women — sago, smoked fish, jungle greens.

14:00

Blowpipe hunting lesson with the village hunters. Try your aim at practice targets.

19:00

Sleep in the uma. Fall asleep to the sound of the jungle at night.

08:00

Witness the sipatiti (shaman) prepare for a healing ceremony. Photography allowed with permission.

10:00

Optional: receive a traditional hand-tapped tattoo from the village tattooist. Takes 1-3 hours depending on design.

14:00

Bark cloth weaving workshop. Learn to pound sago bark into the traditional cloth worn by Sakkudei men.

19:00

Healing ceremony begins. Sit in the longhouse as the shaman works. One of the most extraordinary things you'll witness.

07:00

Dugout canoe journey down river to the coast. Wildlife spotting guaranteed.

11:00

Arrive at a legendary surf break (name kept secret for the group). Surf all afternoon.

17:00

Beach bonfire dinner. The team cooks fresh fish caught that morning.

20:00

Night swim and stargazing in waters with no light pollution.

07:00

Sunrise coconut breakfast. Final gifts exchanged with the village families.

09:00

Return boat to Muara Siberut. Ferry back to Padang.

14:00

Arrive Padang. Group photo, farewells, and the slow re-entry into the modern world.
